Watch out for debris of asteroids and asteroids that bounce back
After your ship respawns, you will have approximately 3 seconds before the game starts dectecting collisions
Each time your ship collides with an asteroid or some debris you will loose one life, you have 3 lives (4 tries)
The goal is to fill up the score bar by destroying asteroids.
The score will slowly decrease as time passes, if it goes back to 0, you lose.
You can fire up to 50 missiles at the same time by pressing the space bar.
After firing all 50 missiles, you'll have to wait 3 seconds before you get another 50.
